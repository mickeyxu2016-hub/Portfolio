About Me

I'm a data professional who bridges the gap between technical analysis and business strategy. My foundation in data science, paired with hands-on experience in business analytics, allows me to not just find patterns in data — but turn them into decisions that matter to stakeholders.

At Kantar China, I analyzed consumer behavior and brand perception for luxury fashion brand Theory, producing data-driven repositioning strategies for the Chinese premium apparel market. Earlier, at DareGlobal, I built sales dashboards tracking overseas market performance across Middle East and South Asia — contributing directly to new client acquisition. I've also built end-to-end cloud data pipelines on AWS, trained ML models for financial risk prediction, and recently shipped an AI-powered natural-language analytics chatbot for enterprise client Komatsu — built on Snowflake Cortex Analyst, Streamlit, and a custom semantic model — as my graduate consulting capstone.

I thrive at the intersection of data, business context, and storytelling — whether that means a clean Tableau dashboard, a well-scoped SQL query, or a concise slide that explains what the numbers actually mean.
